11/10/2002

Maths genius Paul does Malaysia proud By Wan A. Hulaimi (332 wds)
NOW that young Malaysian Mathematics prodigy Paul Nicholas has reached the ivory tower of his dreams at Cambridge University, he is looking even further than the willowy banks of the famous river of his university town. "I'd like to go back to Malaysia to contribute to the country like my grandfather did in his day," he said.

11/10/2002

Pelajar pintar Matematik mahu berbakti By Wan A Hulaimi (233 wds)
LONDON, Khamis - Anak muda berbakat luar biasa dalam bidang Matematik dari Malaysia, Paul Nicholas yang berjaya mencapai impian untuk belajar di Universiti Cambridge, mahu berbakti kepada negara selepas tamat pengajiannya nanti. "Saya ingin kembali ke Malaysia untuk memberi sumbangan kepada negara seperti yang pernah dilakukan oleh datuk saya," katanya.

 

Translation: This maths wizard wishes to contribute his talents to Malaysia.

This young Malaysian boy who has excelled extraordinarily in Maths in London and has fulfilled his dreams of being accepted in to Cambridge wishes to share his talents and contributions following his Grandfather footsteps. He says "I wish to come back to Malaysia and follow in my Grandfathers footsteps and contribute as much as I can to the Malaysian society.

 

Paul
