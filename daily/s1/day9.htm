An example of angelic manifestation



In June 1978, my friend Damien and I attended an International Charismatic conference at the RDS stadium in Dublin, the capital of the Republic of Ireland. One evening when the meeting ended we left with the other 23,000 participants to head into town to catch a bus. We needed to catch a particular bus to reach the home where we were residing in for the course of the whole week.

We were both aware that we were in the company of such a large gathering, that some of them might want to catch the same bus that we were heading for. This day we were extremely exhausted! My friend Damien came out with a preposterous idea that he is going to place his hand out and appeal to a car to stop in this busy roadway, so that the car can take us to the bus stop in the city as quickly as possible. I must admit my faith was not strong that day, but thank God for Damien's faith. For I was shocked that a car actually stopped and it was a lady driver. Just before we reached our destination my curiosity got the better part of me and I needed an answer to this question. I asked the lady driver directly but politely , why did she stop the car for two strangers. She responded by staying, "you are two priests, are you not?" We replied that we were not priests. She said ," the only reason why I stopped the car was that I saw two priests wearing white cassocks standing on the road and I decided to give these two priests a lift." She than looked at us aghast and she noticed we were not wearing white cassocks. She spontaneously told us that if she did not see us as priests, she definitely would not have stopped the car. We than realised that she actually saw with her own eyes our angels standing beside us. This is an awesome thought!!
