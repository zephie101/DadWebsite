It was October 1978 and I was fast asleep at home in Malaysia, which is about 6000 miles from London, England. All of a sudden, between 3am - 3-30 am, I woke up from a deep sleep and I was very troubled. My eyes were than transfixed on this image. It looked like someone had placed the picture of my brother Justin right in front of my eyes. Justin was studying in London. All I could feel at that moment was that his life was in danger. This was a period when there was no such terminology as mobile phones, WhatsApp or anything similar of this nature. I could not contact my brother instantly. There was no Internet whatsoever.

This is an example of a circumstance when one needs to speak in tongues, for one is totally unaware of what's happening. Only my brother Justin and God The Holy Ghost knew of what was really happening at that particular moment. I awoke from my sleep very quickly and started praying in tongues violently.

I was putting on my winter coat, ( my brother had borrowed this coat to keep himself warm whilst I was away), as I placed my hands inside the pocket I noticed that there was an underground Tube ticket. I took this out and I observed it was an October date. Then it dawned on me that it was the same date that I was woken up from my deep sleep when I was in Malaysia. I felt I had to urgently quiz my brother and ask him what happened on this date. When I questioned him he was very evasive and he was not prepared to give me any answers. I had to explain to him the whole scenario of what happened in Malaysia. Only than he volunteered the information. This was a Friday night, he and some of his friends were walking in the streets in North London and some very violent thugs pounced on them and began to beat and punch them, which was an unprovoked attack. They severely attacked my brother. This was the time, when all this violence was being perpetrated, unknown to me, I was woken up from my sleep in Malaysia, thousands of miles away and began to pray in tongues violently.

When my brother was taken to the casualty department in the hospital, the Doctors were amazed and dumbfounded, given the severity of the beating that there was no internal bleeding and there were no fractures in his body and they discharged him. Oh, thank God, The Holy Ghost saved his life.
