An introduction to Pastor Kingsley Appiagyei and his church, Trinity Baptist Church.

We received at short notice a tender invitation, in December 2024, from Trinity Baptist Church Croydon to attend the funeral service of our very loving friend Mrs Victoria Jane Asare Amosah.

We know Victoria as the wife to Reverend Kweku Amosah.
He was a leader during the time when the late Nicholas Rivett Carnac and John Starr were the Vicars at St. Marks Church, Kennington.London.

We know the Amosahs very well. During the course of our spiritual journey we did attend Trinity Baptist Church and were mightily challenged and blessed.

What a glorious funeral. A precious home going.

It was conducted as for a farewell service befitting a 'queen'. The Praise and Worship was Throne Room level.

For the last two years,Dec'2002 and Dec'2003, we felt it was somewhat mandatory from a prompting of God The Holy Spirit to attend the New Year Eve service at Trinity Baptist church,located in West Norwood, South London. This church has a congregation of more than a thousand people. It is during this time that the Pastor, Kingsley Appiagyei delivers his vision message, i.e. which is usually a prophetic message in regard to a dawning of a New Year. The service lasts for a long time, usually between 8 pm till 1am. We enjoy every minute of it. The praise and worship is superb. Very moving. The Presence of God is tangible, for God The Holy Ghost manifests His Presence. This is one of the many reasons why we like to attend the New Year service in this church. For the Praise and Worship is done in a reverential manner and this enables us to sense the Presence of God. We come back home at about 2am feeling that we have encountered a lasting experience with God.

Our hunger to get to know our Lord must be strong. I remember once sitting quietly in the local church, St. James the Great R.C. Church, Peckham Rye, in London, England, on 3rd April 1993. My mind was distracted in wanting to follow my own ambitions and desires.

I sensed the voice of Our Heavenly Father ministering to me and instructing me, “Why don’t you, Anton, pursue My Son and pursue Him intensely?"

From that day on I decided to pursue this instruction meticulously.

I had already graduated with a Banking Diploma from IFS University College,UK.

I came back home and gathered every single banking text book I owned plus all paraphernalia relating to my potential successful career. I placed them neatly in a black garbage bag and threw them away. From then henceforth my challenging spiritual journey commenced.
