Reading for Day 41: A Prayer

 

What Must I Give You, Lord, For Being So Good To Me!

Father God, I am praying, asking of You, enquiring from You of what is written within:

Psalm 116:12

12) What must I give you, LORD,

for being so good to me?

 

In Return May I speak forth and witness openly regarding:

Psalm 145:

6) I will tell of the power of your awesome works,
and I will proclaim your great deeds.

7) I will celebrate your abundant goodness
and joyfully sing of your righteousness.

8 ) The LORD is gracious and compassionate,
slow to anger and rich in love.

9) The LORD is good to all;
he has compassion on all he has made.

 

and decree with utmost conviction the words of Psalm 90, which was originally prayed by Moses the man of God:

17) May the favour of the Lord our God rest upon us;
establish the work of our hands for us—
yes, establish the work of our hands.

 

 

And we desire Heavenly Father to grow, nurture, shape and conform to this life sustaining sayings:

Colossians 3:12-16

12) Therefore, as God's chosen people, holy and dearly loved, clothe yourselves with compassion, kindness, humility, gentleness and patience.

13) Bear with each other and forgive whatever grievances you may have against one another. Forgive as the Lord forgave you. 14) And over all these virtues put on love, which binds them all together in perfect unity.

And this is what we desire, strive and aim for Father, The peace of Christ must reign supreme in our lives

Colossians 3:

15) Let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, since as members of one body you were called to peace. And be thankful.

16) Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly as you teach and admonish one another with all wisdom, and as you sing psalms, hymns and spiritual songs with gratitude in your hearts to God.

Thank You Father God for answering our prayers

Psalm 66:20

Praise God, who did not ignore my prayer
and did not withdraw his unfailing love from me.

 

And finally Father I will memorize, meditate,( think upon) and visualize on this verse:

In Adoration

2 Chronicles 6

12) Then Solomon stood with his hands spread out before the altar of the LORD in front of the entire community of Israel.

14) He prayed, "O LORD, God of Israel, there is no God like you in all of heaven and earth. You keep your promises and show unfailing love to all who obey you and are eager to do your will

 

Not withstanding the fact that I have not forgotten these scripture:

Lamentations 3

25)  The LORD is good to those whose hope is in him,
       to the one who seeks him

 

Psalm 16

7)  I will bless the LORD who guides me;
       even at night my heart instructs me.

    8)   I know the LORD is always with me.
       I will not be shaken, for he is right beside me
