Malaysia Day/ Merdeka    

The gathering came from all over the United Kingdom.                         

On 28th August 2004 we ( a more recent photograph and the latest news of our family is at the bottom of this page)  attended a festival in Hertfordshire, England, being held to celebrate Merdeka (Independence Day) for Malaysia. We had a great time there and especially enjoyed the food! The venue was the Tun Abdul Razak Research Centre (TARRC), Brickendonbury, Hertford, Hertfordshire SG13 8NL.

We enjoyed ourselves eating chapatti rotis, but very sadly missed out on durians! This is the first time we celebrated Merdeka Day in the UK. Our thanks go to Mr Azam Ashari for informing us of this event.
