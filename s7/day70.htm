Joyful Christmas / Glorious New Year 2025/2026

To Our Dear Friends,

What a fascinating and fast-moving year it has been for us.

Something happened to Anton just two days before Charlie Kirk of Turning Point USA was assassinated on 10th September 2025 in Orem, Utah, USA. The dates are significant here: 9th, 10th, 11th, and 12th September 2025.

It was on 9th September 2025 that God the Father prepared Anton to receive the tragic news. It all happened on a London bus (route 197) travelling from Penge, London, to Croydon, Surrey. For further incredible details please view the video.

I received a prophetic word for 2026 on that very bus.

This brought forward my first YouTube video, which was relayed from our own miraculous chapel (now my studio).

Our Lord astounded me when the video, once published for the world to see, received more than 900 views in a single day.

It was a serious and urgent word that I had to pray over with all fervour and zeal.

The scripture that came to my attention to confirm the word was found in Luke 24:30–32:

30 When he was at the table with them, he took bread, gave thanks, broke it and began to give it to them.
31 Then their eyes were opened and they recognised him, and he disappeared from their sight.
32 They asked each other, “Were not our hearts burning within us while he talked with us on the road and opened the Scriptures to us?”

So, in the year 2026, I pray that He will open our eyes in an extraordinary way, so much so that our hearts will burn within us and the Scriptures will be embedded in us.

As I speak the living Word, the scriptural verses will manifest in power and strength.

What does this mean? Please check it out through these short reel videos, published for the first time in 2025, on the link below:

They came about when our dear Lord opened my eyes and mind to see Jesus Christ as He truly is.

We are living in very troubling and distressing times. We have been called to pray for the United Kingdom.

The call to prayer is coming from many quarters.
We are responding. I have been preparing for this call for many years.

So we lift up a prayer for you all in the year 2026—a year of painful, tumultuous shakings. As far as possible, stay secured in “Goshen territory”—meaning, stay close to our Lord, as Anton does in his room through the early hours of the night, receiving assurances that his family and friends are safe and sound before he finally goes to bed.

Many, many blessings,

Anton & Saida Nicholas and Valerie Meilton
